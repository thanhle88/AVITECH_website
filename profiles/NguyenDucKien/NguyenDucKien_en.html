Nguyen Duc Kien received his B.Sc. in Electronics and Communications Engineering Technology from the University of Engineering and Technology, Vietnam National University, Hanoi (VNU-UET) in 2024, and is currently pursuing an M.Sc. in Electronics Engineering at VNU-UET. He is involved in a collaborative research project between Bach Mai Hospital (Hanoi) and research groups in Brussels, focusing on cognitive deterioration mechanisms in neuroinflammatory diseases. His work aims to develop a comprehensive understanding of brain disorders, multimodal brain data, and interdisciplinary research approaches.
