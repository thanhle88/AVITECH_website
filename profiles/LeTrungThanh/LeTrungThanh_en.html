Thanh Trung LE is currently a university lecturer (equiv. assistant professor) at the VNU University of Engineering and Technology, Vietnam National University Hanoi (VNU-UET). He obtained his B.Sc. and M.Sc. degrees in Electronics and Communications from VNU-UET in 2016 and 2018, respectively. In 2022, he completed his Ph.D. degree in Computer Science (Informatique) from the University of Orléans, INSA CVL, PRISME, France. Following his Ph.D., he continued his research as a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Orléans from 2022 to 2023 His research interests include topics of applied mathematics, signal processing, optimization, data science, and their applications: + Matrix/tensor decomposition and analysis + Low-rank approximation, estimation and tracking + Online and adaptive optimization methods + System identification + Biomedical data processing
