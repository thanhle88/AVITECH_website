• Cyber Security Engineer at the University of Canberra, Australia

Tran Viet Khoa received his B.Sc. degree in electronics and telecommunications from the University of Engineering and Technology, Vietnam National University (VNU-UET), in 2008, followed by an M.Sc. degree in Information, Systems, and Technology from Paris-Sud 11, France, in 2010. He received a Ph.D. degree at the University of Technology Sydney (UTS), Australia, in 2024. He is currently working as a Cybersecurity Engineer at the University of Canberra, Australia. His research interests span across cyberattack detection, deep learning, federated learning, different privacy, IoT and blockchain.
