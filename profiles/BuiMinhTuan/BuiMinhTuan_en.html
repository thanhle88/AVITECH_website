• Researcher at Academy of Science and Technology

Dr. Bui Minh Tuan received the Ph.D. degree in Electrical and Data Engineering from the University of Technology Sydney (UTS), Australia. He is currently with the Academy of Military Science and Technology, Hanoi, Vietnam. His research interests include wireless communications, physical-layer security, integrated sensing and communication (ISAC), and deep learning for communication systems.
