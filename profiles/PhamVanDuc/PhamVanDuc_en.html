Pham Van Duc received the Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) degree in Computer Engineering from the VNU University of Engineering and Technology (VNU-UET), Vietnam National University, Hanoi, Vietnam, in 2025. He is currently pursuing the Master’s degree in Electronics Engineering at VNU-UET. His research interests include advanced signal processing (with a focus on Doppler radar) and thermal imaging analysis for the extraction of vital signs such as heart rate, respiratory rate, and blood pressure.
